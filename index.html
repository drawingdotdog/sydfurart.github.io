SydFur Creatives

About | Directory

Welcome to one of the queerest, furriest slices of the net around! We're an 18+ social group for furry artists, writers & other creatives in and around Sydney, Australia. We meet up monthly to practice our crafts, share & learn together.

Our community follows a pretty simple set of rules, and if you want to be listed on the directory you'll have to agree to follow them:

  1. Don't be an asshole (transphobia, misogyny, body shaming, etc will not be tolerated)
  2. No AI art / writing / etc (if you want that, find a different group)
