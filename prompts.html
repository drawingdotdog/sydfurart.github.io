For 2025 we're running the #SydFurMonthly prompt competition!

Every month an animal, location/setting, verb pair & palette will be provided. Anyone who creates something that uses at least two of the prompts and shares it with the group, will gain a raffle ticket!

The raffle will run through the entirety of the year, will be drawn on 1/1/2026, and for fairness each participant can only win a single prize (but submitting multiple works will increase your chances of being drawn)

You can submit a prompt from any month, at any time throughout the year! The spirit of this competition is to encourage creativity rather than to make a challenge with strict deadlines ^_^

The current prizes are:

  • 4x $20 vouchers to either Eckersleys, Parkers Art Supplies or a similar creative business of your choice (so long as they're local or provide an option for purchasing digital vouchers)

If that sounds like fun to you, come join us and share your creations!

To enter your work into the competition, all you have to do is post it in our Telegram group and tag it with #SydFurMonthly
